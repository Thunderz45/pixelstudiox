Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

LeadFlow Agent ("the Extension") is developed by Pixel Studio X. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the Extension.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension may collect the following information only to provide its intended functionality:

  • User authentication information necessary to verify access to the LeadFlow platform.
  • Business lead information extracted from supported websites after the user initiates the extraction process.
  • User-selected project information required to organize collected leads.
  • Temporary extension preferences and settings necessary for operation.

The Extension does not collect:

  • Health information
  • Financial or payment information
  • Personal communications
  • Precise location data
  • Unrelated browsing history
  • Keystrokes or screen recordings

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate users.
  • Extract business lead information requested by the user.
  • Save collected leads to the user's LeadFlow account.
  • Synchronize data between the extension and the LeadFlow platform.
  • Maintain extension functionality.

3. Data Storage

Lead information and user account data are securely stored on LeadFlow servers. Temporary operational settings may be stored locally only when necessary for extension functionality.

4. Cookies

The Extension may access authentication cookies solely to maintain a secure signed-in session with the LeadFlow platform. Cookies are never used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

We do not share user data with third parties except when required to operate the LeadFlow service or when required by law.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect transmitted and stored data. Communication between the Extension and LeadFlow servers is encrypted using HTTPS.

7. User Control

Users may stop using the Extension at any time by uninstalling it. Users may also request deletion of their LeadFlow account and associated data through our support channels.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Website: https://pixelstudiox.in

Email: support@pixelstudiox.in
